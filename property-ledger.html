  • Emergency fund (25%): your manually-set emergency amount compared to 6× your monthly take-home salary. Full marks at 100%+ of that target.
  • Savings rate (25%): your "Savings / investments" monthly entry as a percentage of take-home income, benchmarked against a commonly cited 20% target.
  • Spending balance (25%): groceries, transport, and utilities ("needs") kept at or under ~50% of income, and subscriptions, cosmetics, leisure, and other ("wants") at or under ~30% — the classic 50/30/20 rule, savings being the remaining 20%.
  • Housing affordability (25%): each property's total monthly cost (mortgage + LPT + management + insurances) as a share of income, checked against a commonly used ≤35% guideline. This one's shown per-property since it depends which you choose, rather than folded into the single score above.
